# Breathe Skill Meditation Analogy

**Date:** 2026-03-21
**Type:** insight
**Status:** extracted

## The Analogy:

The breathe skill follows meditation breathing practice:

- **Inhale** → Archive (take in experiences)
- **Hold** → Distill (contemplate/process thoughts)
- **Exhale** → Weave (release insights as learnings)
- **Rest** → Sync (commit to neurograph, mind clear)

## Like Box Breathing:

Inhale 5 → Hold 5 → Exhale 5 → Hold empty 5 → Calm nervous system, clear mental clutter

## The Brain Clear Practice:

Meditation is to clear your mind. Similarly, breathing clears the session context bloat. Session rotation proposal: auto-spawn new session after breathe completes, giving fresh context without accumulated conversation weight.

This analogy makes the technical pipeline intuitive and memorable for users.
