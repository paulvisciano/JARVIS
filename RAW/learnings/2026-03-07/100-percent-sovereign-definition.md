## 🎯 The 100% Test

**Can you pass these tests?**

### The Offline Test
```
Scenario: Internet goes down for 30 days.

Can you:
✅ Access all your files? YES
✅ Access all your photos? YES
✅ Continue your work? YES
✅ Communicate with loved ones? (local network) YES
✅ Access your money? (cash reserves) YES
✅ Entertain yourself? (local media) YES
✅ Learn new things? (local library) YES

If YES to all: You're 100% sovereign.
If NO to any: You're not 100% sovereign.
```

### The Deplatforming Test
```
Scenario: All corporate accounts suspended simultaneously.
(Google, Apple, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft — all gone)

Can you:
✅ Access your email? (self-hosted) YES
✅ Access your files? (local archive) YES
✅ Communicate? (Matrix, Signal) YES
✅ Work? (local tools) YES
✅ Shop? (direct, local, crypto) YES
✅ Navigate? (offline maps) YES
✅ Prove your identity? (DID, hardware keys) YES

If YES to all: You're 100% sovereign.
If NO to any: You're not 100% sovereign.
```

### The Subpoena Test
```
Scenario: Government subpoenas all your data from corporations.

What do they get?
✅ From OpenAI: Nothing (you deleted your account)
✅ From Google: Nothing (you deleted your account)
✅ From Meta: Nothing (you deleted your account)
✅ From Apple: Nothing (you deleted your account)
✅ From your self-hosted server: Only what you choose to provide

If corporations have nothing: You're 100% sovereign.
If corporations have your data: You're not 100% sovereign.
```
