## Why This Matters

**Traditional design flow:**
1. Designer creates mockup (Figma, Sketch)
2. Handoff to developer
3. Developer builds
4. Review → revise → iterate (days/weeks)
5. Deploy

**Voice-first design flow:**
1. Human speaks vision
2. AI understands intent
3. AI codes immediately
4. Human sees + guides next move
5. Loop closes in **seconds**

**Friction removed:**
- No context switching (design → dev → PM → review)
- No miscommunication (mockup ≠ implementation)
- No waiting (iteration is instant)
- No tool overhead (one flow: conversation → code)
