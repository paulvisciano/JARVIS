# Knowledge Origami Architecture

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Type:** insight
**Status:** extracted

Knowledge is folded into three layers for varying recall needs:
1. **Learnings:** Full detail (individual .md files per insight)
2. **Summary:** Quick scan (living digest of the day)
3. **Analogies:** Instant meaning (essence in metaphor)

This compression allows efficient retrieval while preserving depth. The origami cycle unfolds when curiosity signals a gap, then refolds with richer context.
