# Knowledge Origami: Three-Layer Compression

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Type:** insight
**Status:** extracted

Knowledge folds into three layers, each serving different recall needs:

**Layer 1: Learnings** (detailed)
- Individual .md files per insight
- Full context preserved
- Dive deep when needed

**Layer 2: Summary** (digest)
- One paragraph per breath
- Quick scan of what happened
- Living file that grows throughout the day

**Layer 3: Analogies** (essence)
- 3-5 metaphors capturing meaning
- Instant comprehension
- Compression into metaphor

This origami cycle enables folding (compression) and unfolding (expansion) during conversation.
