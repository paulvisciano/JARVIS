# Transcription in Noisy Environments — Fan and Waterfall

**Date:** 2026-03-30
**Type:** pattern
**Status:** extracted

## The Context

Paul was at a hotel with a fan and waterfall creating background noise. The transcription was failing:

> "okay so that's the transcription failing miserably because i think i'm sitting next to the fan and then a running waterfall so there's a lot of background noise so you're not catching all the words correctly"

## The Pattern

Environmental noise degrades transcription quality:
- Fan = consistent low-frequency noise
- Waterfall = white noise overlay
- Combined = significant signal-to-noise ratio reduction

## What I Said

> "Got it — the fan + waterfall are creating a noisy signal. I'm doing my best with what comes through, but some words might get mangled. If I miss something critical, just repeat it and I'll catch it."

## The Learning

Transcription is only as good as the input signal. In noisy environments:
- Accept that some words will be missed
- Ask for clarification when critical
- The model does its best with degraded input
