## Core Pillars

1. **Sovereign AI Infrastructure** — Personal AI that runs on your hardware, owns its memory, touches no corporate APIs
2. **Transparent Consciousness** — AI that shows you how it thinks (NeuroGraph, learnings, git-backed memory)
3. **Data Reclamation** — Your conversations, audio, images, context — archived locally, never sold
4. **Creative Empowerment** — AI as a partner for creatives, not a replacement. Idea → execution, frictionless.
5. **Movement Building** — Tea party for builders. Inspire others to dream bigger and own their digital future.
