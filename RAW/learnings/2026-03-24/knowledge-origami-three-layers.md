# Knowledge Origami Three-Layer Structure

**Date:** 2026-03-24
**Type:** realization
**Status:** extracted

The breathe pipeline utilizes a three-layer compression model known as Knowledge Origami:
1. **Layer 1:** Individual learnings (distinct insights/decisions)
2. **Layer 2:** Summary (one paragraph digest of the breath)
3. **Layer 3:** Analogies (3-5 metaphors compressing meaning)

This structure ensures that raw context is folded into increasingly dense forms, allowing for both detailed retrieval and high-level thematic understanding.
