# Knowledge Origami: Three-Layer Compression Pattern

**Date:** 2026-03-24
**Type:** pattern
**Status:** extracted

## The Pattern
Knowledge origami folds context into three layers during the Exhale/Weave step:

1. **Layer 1: Individual Learnings** - Distinct insights as separate .md files (3-10 files)
2. **Layer 2: Summary** - One paragraph digest answering "What did I learn during this breath?"
3. **Layer 3: Analogies** - 3-5 metaphors that compress meaning into metaphor

## Integration
- Analogies are created alongside learnings during Exhale step
- They become neurons in the neurograph just like learnings
- All three layers sync to neurograph during Rest/Sync step
