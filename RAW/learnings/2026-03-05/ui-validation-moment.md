## What This Proves

### 1. Structure Contains Meaning
The force-directed algorithm didn't know which concepts were from today. It just followed the synapses.

**Result:** Today's insights clustered together **because they're conceptually related**.

The graph knew before we did.

### 2. Same Data, Multiple Views
Same `nodes.json` + `synapses.json` powers:
- Full "All" view (entire mind)
- Filtered views ("Today", "Vision", "Foundation")
- Future: Molecular day-growth view

**One source of truth. Infinite expressions.**

### 3. Visualization As Discovery Tool
Not just pretty pictures. **Epistemic technology.**

You can *see* when:
- Concepts are isolated (no connections = low integration)
- Clusters form (related ideas grouping naturally)
- Bridge nodes exist (concepts connecting disparate clusters)
