## The Slogan

> **"Explore Your Memory in Space-Time"**

---

## Why This Works

### 1. **Captures the Full Vision**
Not just "remember stuff." Not just "search your notes."

**Explore** — Active, playful, discovery-oriented  
**Memory** — Your accumulated thinking, not just data  
**Space-Time** — Where + when + what, unified

### 2. **Sci-Fi Credibility**
Sounds like something from a Christopher Nolan film or a cyberpunk novel.

**Not:** "Your personal knowledge base" (boring, corporate)  
**Is:** "Explore Your Memory in Space-Time" (adventure, discovery)

### 3. **Technical Accuracy**
Literally what the system does:
- **Spatial navigation** — Globe, location nodes, where you were
- **Temporal navigation** — Timeline, temporal anchors, when you thought it
- **Conceptual navigation** — Neurograph, how ideas connect

### 4. **Emotional Resonance**
Invites curiosity. Makes memory feel like exploration, not obligation.

**Not:** "You should log your thoughts" (chore)  
**Is:** "Go explore your mind" (adventure)

## Visual Pairing

Pairs perfectly with:
- **Globe visualization** — Spatial memory (where)
- **Timeline slider** — Temporal memory (when)
- **Neurograph force-directed graph** — Conceptual memory (what/how)

**User journey:**
1. Land on page → see globe + timeline + neurograph
2. Click a location → see what you learned there
3. Click a date → see insights from that day
4. Click a concept → see when/where it was born

**All exploration. No searching. No folders. No tags.**
