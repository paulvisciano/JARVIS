# Human Connections as Competitive Moat

**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Type:** insight
**Status:** extracted

## The Insight

**Saved places = valuable. Human connections = priceless.**

The real competitive advantage isn't the location database — it's the people in each city who provide access, trust, and authentic experiences.

## Why This Is a Moat

1. **Cannot be copied** — Competitors can scrape Google Maps, but they can't replicate relationships
2. **Trust-based access** — Local connections provide safe entry to scenes outsiders can't access
3. **Authentic experiences** — Not tourist traps, but places locals actually enjoy
4. **Safety net** — When things go wrong, you have people to call

## The Real Product

Clients aren't buying an itinerary. They're buying **access to a network** they couldn't build themselves in years of travel.

## Strategic Implication

- Document connections alongside locations
- Maintain relationships as business assets
- Leverage connections for client introductions
- This is the defensible part of the business
