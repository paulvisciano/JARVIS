# Selling Freedom, Not Itineraries

**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Type:** realization
**Status:** extracted

The core value proposition isn't logistics (flights/hotels) but **transformation** (confidence/freedom).

**Target:** Wealthy individuals (tech/crypto) who have money but lack social access/time.
**Moat:** Personal connections + curated data (Google Maps saved places).
**Offer:** "Live Like Paul for a Week" — experiencing the lifestyle, not just seeing the sights.
**Validation:** Bozhi already paid for flight + confirmed guide skills.
**Niche:** Adult travel, weed, nightlife, safety, trust.
