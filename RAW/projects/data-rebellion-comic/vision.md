## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Art Quality Mismatch
**Problem:** Hired artist doesn't deliver cyberpunk aesthetic.  
**Mitigation:** 
- Portfolio review (must have sci-fi/cyberpunk work)
- Paid test page (1 page before full contract)
- Milestone payments (not upfront, protect both parties)
- Kill fee clause (can exit if quality drops)

### Risk 2: Scope Creep
**Problem:** 48 pages balloons to 80+, budget explodes.  
**Mitigation:**
- Locked script before art starts (no mid-stream changes)
- Buffer in budget (10% contingency)
- Clear change order process (Paul approves all additions)
- MVP mindset (Issue #1 tells complete arc, sequels expand)

### Risk 3: Kickstarter Failure
**Problem:** Doesn't reach funding goal.  
**Mitigation:**
- Build email list BEFORE launch (1,000+ subscribers target)
- Line up "day 1 backers" (friends, community, 30% goal secured)
- Have backup plan (self-fund smaller print run if needed)
- All-or-nothing creates urgency (use it in marketing)

### Risk 4: Legal Issues (Corporate Depictions)
**Problem:** Anthropic/Google/Meta threaten legal action.  
**Mitigation:**
- Faceless corporations (towers, no logos)
- Parody/fair use (commentary, not trademark infringement)
- Legal review before print (one-time consult, $500-1K)
- Disclaimers ("fictionalized, for educational purposes")
